## 获取用户交易记录(分页)
- 币对 California Carbon Allowance Vintage 2022 Future <> usdt
    - California Carbon Allowance Vintage 2022 Future -> usdt
       query MyQuery {
        swaps(
        skip: 0
        first: 10
        where: {pair_: {id: "0xa58ca32b40de2d1be8818968a58182ee86b2df7a"}, amount1In_gt: "0"}
        ) {
        sender
        amount0In
        amount0Out
        amount1In
        amount1Out
        timestamp
        }
